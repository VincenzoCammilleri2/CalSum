What is CalSum?

CalSum is a easy-to-use calculator that anyone can use to determine the calories that they need to 'maintain' their current weight, how many calories to lose or see a 'deficit', and how many calories to gain or 'bulk'.

  1. Maintenance: calories to eat or keep eating in order to 'maintain' their current body weight. Knowing this value is crucial in order to determine goals and how to achieve it.
  2. Deficit: how many calories to lose weight. Done by taking Maintenance, which is what would keep the current weight, and subtract 300-500 calories from that.
  3. Bulk: how many calories to gain weight. Done by taking Maintenance, which is what would keep the current weight, and adding 300-500 calories to that.

What is BMR?

"Basal metabolic rate is the number of calories your body needs to accomplish its most basic (basal) life-sustaining functions." - Scott Frothingham via healthline
